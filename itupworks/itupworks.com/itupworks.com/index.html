Get Enlightened with Us

The unfulfilled job opportunities & jobless candidates led to the origination of ITUpworks. This existing imbalance and challenges faced by educational institutes to fine-tune their curriculum per industry requirements made us ponder over the reasons and the solution to it.

We provide the right training platform and the skills required to carve out the desired profile in the industry. We not only groom the candidates per industry requirements but also help them choose the right career path in-line with their interests and capabilities.

Not just the candidates, we also have our service portfolio catering to Professionals, Institutions and Corporate as well.

Program Methodology



Know the candidates based on assessment template covering vast number of parameters and algorithm to judge and define the baseline (existing) skill set.



Rapidly build and execute custom skill development programs bridging gap between the individual skill sets and the industry needs, thereby carving a perfect IT Profile.



Expose candidates to the real business world and give them required practical experience to ensure right alignment of their skills with Industry needs.

With this, we assure a great Career Start and Corporate Success !
